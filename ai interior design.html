AI Interior Design System (Full VR Styling)

Experience the future of spatial design with our AI-powered interior transformation system, built entirely in 3D.

Users can dynamically change wall materials, colors, lighting types, layouts, and furniture styles — all in real time. The system analyzes spatial context and user prompts to generate personalized interior options instantly.

Built for VR using Unity + C#, with AI-driven presets and interactions, this system is being fully integrated into the VR experience for Meta Quest 3, enabling immersive, hands-on interior design like never before.

ENGINE: Unity 2023 LTS + XR Toolkit

PLATFORM: PCVR Demo, Meta Quest 3 (In Progress)

AI FEATURES: Wall & Floor Materials, Dynamic Lighting, Layout Optimization, Style Matching

TECH STACK: Unity, GPT-4 API, AI Material Generator, Prompt-Driven Design UI
