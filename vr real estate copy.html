VR Real Estate Walkthrough

We turn 2D architectural floor plans into fully immersive 3D virtual homes using **Unity and ProBuilder.

Every home is built from the ground up — walls, dimensions, and structure are accurately modeled, then styled with realistic furniture, textures, and lighting.

Clients can walk through their dream home in VR before it’s even built. This helps interior designers, architects, and real estate agents communicate vision, close deals faster, and reduce revisions.

BUILT WITH: Unity + ProBuilder

PLATFORMS: Meta Quest, WebXR, PCVR

PIPELINE: Floor Plan → ProBuilder → Interior Design → VR Deployment

SUITABLE FOR: Real Estate Agents, Builders, Interior Designers
